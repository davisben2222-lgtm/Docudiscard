Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I set up the AI Analysis?

To use the "Analyze with AI" feature, you must provide your own OpenAI API key. Navigate to the API tab within the app and paste your secret key. This ensures you only pay for the AI processing you actually use.

2. How do I manage my subscription?

DocuDiscard offers 1 free item per job. To unlock unlimited items and professional PDF exports, you can subscribe to our Premium plan. Subscriptions are managed securely through your Apple ID Settings.

3. Can I export my reports to other apps?

Yes. Once a job is complete, go to the Settings tab to generate and share your text or PDF reports via email, cloud storage, or messaging apps.
