Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

DocuDiscard is committed to protecting the privacy of restoration professionals and their clients. This policy outlines how we handle data within the app.

1. Data Collection and Storage

We believe in data ownership. All job details, client information, and item photos are stored locally on your device or within your private iCloud storage.We do not host your claim data on our own servers. User prompts are shared with Google Gemini to provide AI responses, but no personal identifiers are attached.

2. AI Analysis & Third-Party Processing

The "Analyze with AI" feature utilizes the OpenAI API. When you initiate an analysis:

  • Selected photos are sent to Gemini to generate descriptions and valuations.
  • This data is processed according to Gemini’s API Privacy Policy.
  • Your Gemini API Key is stored securely on your device and is never shared with us.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

All premium subscriptions are processed through Apple In-App Purchases. We do not collect or store your credit card numbers or billing addresses. Your transaction data is managed entirely by Apple.

4. Data Security

Since data is stored locally, the security of your documentation depends on your device's security settings (Passcode, FaceID, and iCloud encryption). We recommend using device encryption to protect sensitive claim information.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: docudiscard@gmail.com]
